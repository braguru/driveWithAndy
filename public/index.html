Ghana Travel Essentials

Everything you need to know for a seamless entry into Ghana.

Visa & Entry

E-visa available for all African nationals (free as of May 2026). Most visitors require a standard visa via gisonline.gov.gh.

Health & Vaccinations

Yellow Fever certificate is mandatory for entry. Malaria prophylaxis (e.g., Malarone) is strongly recommended. Drink bottled water only.

Cultural Etiquette

Akwaaba (Welcome) is central to Ghanaian culture. Greet with the right hand. Modest dress is respectful for religious or rural visits.
